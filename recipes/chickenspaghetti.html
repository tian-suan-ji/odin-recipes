Chicken Spaghetti


Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 package bacon bits
  • 1 20 oz can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 20 oz can cream of chicken soup
  • 3/4 block Velveeta Easy Melt cheese cubed
  • 1 package rigatoni pasta
  • 1 small bag shredded Parmesan cheese
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook rigatoni in boiling water for about 8 minutes until al dente drain and set aside
  2. Cook chicken in a large skillet over medium high heat season with garlic powder salt and pepper cook until fully done then remove from skillet and cut into large cubes
  3. Add cream of mushroom soup cream of chicken soup and milk to the same skillet
  4. Add cubed Velveeta cheese and stir gently to combine reduce heat to medium and let cheese melt while stirring
  5. Add chicken back into the skillet and mix
  6. Turn off heat and add cooked rigatoni stir to combine
  7. Add Parmesan cheese and bacon bits and mix then serve
