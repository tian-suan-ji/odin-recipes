Sweet Tea

Ingredients

  • 4 tea bags (black tea)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat and add the tea bags.
  3. Let the tea steep for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Remove the tea bags.
  5. Add the sugar while the tea is still hot and stir until completely dissolved.
  6. Pour the tea into a pitcher.
  7. Add the remaining 4 cups of cold water.
  8. Stir well.
  9. Refrigerate until chilled.
  10. Serve over ice.
